The 4 zones of the Inline Advisor:

1. Proactive Insight Cards
Auto-generated alerts: spending spikes, savings, anomalies, risks. Scrollable horizontal strip.
2. Smart Chart
Context-aware visualization that changes based on time period and filters. Bar/pie/trend toggle. AI annotation underneath.
3. Ask Bar
Command palette-style input. Natural language questions. Quick suggestion chips.
4. Agent Response
Rich responses with inline charts, data tables, and action buttons. Same output goes to Telegram.
