## Connect your bank

Connecting a bank is the fastest way to keep your books current — transactions flow in and the agent matches them to invoices and expenses for you.

### How to connect

1. Go to **Expenses → Bank accounts** (or ask the agent to *"connect my bank"*).
2. Choose your institution and sign in through the secure bank connection window.
3. Approve read-only access. AgentBook never sees or stores your bank password.

Your access token is encrypted at rest, and you can disconnect an account at any time.

### What happens after connecting

- Recent transactions import automatically, and a first-time connection backfills up to a couple of years of history so your past year reconstructs.
- The agent auto-categorizes each transaction and matches payments to open invoices.
- Anything it's unsure about lands in your review queue instead of being guessed.

### Good to know

- **Bank connection is a paid feature** — you'll be prompted to upgrade the first time you connect.
- Personal and business accounts can both be linked; mark transactions as business or personal and the agent keeps them separate for tax.
