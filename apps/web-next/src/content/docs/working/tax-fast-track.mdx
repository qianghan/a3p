## Tax fast-track filing

Instead of starting this year's filing from a blank page, fast-track starts from last year's **confirmed return** and only asks about what's different. A few quick questions — in chat or on the **Fast-Track** tab under **Tax & Reports** — and the agent drafts a full estimate plus a cover letter you can hand straight to your accountant.

### Starting one

Ask the agent directly:

- *"Start my tax fast-track filing."*
- *"Is my filing draft ready?"* checks status on an in-progress one.

Or open the **Fast-Track** tab and click **Start**. Either way it's the same session — pick it up wherever's convenient.

### What it asks

The questions adapt to your jurisdiction and to what your prior return already tells it — it skips anything it can already answer. A few examples:

- **US** — filing status changes, new W-2/1099 income, itemizing vs. the standard deduction, retirement contributions.
- **Canada** — province of residence, T4/T4A/T5 slips, RRSP/FHSA/TFSA contributions, the GST/HST registration threshold.
- **Australia** — sole trader vs. company/partnership/trust, the GST registration threshold, superannuation contributions, private health insurance and the Medicare Levy Surcharge.

See the full country-by-country breakdown in [Supported countries](/docs/regions/overview).

### What you get

- **A filing draft** — an estimated total income, taxable income, and tax payable, computed by the same real tax-bracket tables used everywhere else in AgentBook — never guessed by the AI.
- **A client letter** — a plain-English cover letter summarizing what changed, the estimated figures, and open questions for your accountant to double-check. Download it as a PDF alongside the draft.
- **Deadline nudges** — a countdown to your actual filing deadline shows on the tab, and the agent proactively reminds you as it gets close.

> **An estimate, not a filing.** Fast-track prepares a draft and a letter to speed up your actual filing — it doesn't file anything for you. Confirm the numbers with your accountant (or your own filing software) before you rely on them.

### Availability

Tax fast-track is a paid add-on ($49/year in the US, $65/year in Canada, $59/year in Australia) — enable it under **Settings → Billing**.
