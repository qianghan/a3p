## Connect Claude via MCP

AgentBook works as a **Model Context Protocol (MCP) server**, so you can access your books and manage finances directly from Claude or any MCP-compatible client. Chat with your bookkeeper in one place.

### What you can do

Once connected, Claude can:

- **Query your books** — "What's my expense summary this month?" "How much tax should I set aside?"
- **Record transactions** — "Log $50 coffee" "Create an invoice for $2,000 to Acme Inc"
- **Analyze finances** — "Show me my vendor breakdown" "What's my cash flow looking like?"
- **Draft plans** — "What if I buy a $3,000 laptop?" "How much can I spend this quarter?"

### Add AgentBook to Claude

#### Option 1: Claude Desktop

1. Open **Claude Desktop Settings** (gear icon, bottom-left)
2. Go to **Connectors**
3. Click **+ Add custom connector**
4. Paste this URL: `https://agentbook.brainliber.com/api/v1/mcp`
5. Click **Add**
6. You'll be redirected to log in with your AgentBook account
7. Grant Claude access to your books
8. Done — Claude now has access to your AgentBook data

#### Option 2: Claude API / Web

1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and open **Settings**
2. Navigate to **Connectors**
3. Click **+ Add custom connector**
4. Paste this URL: `https://agentbook.brainliber.com/api/v1/mcp`
5. Follow the login and approval flow
6. Start chatting — Claude will suggest AgentBook actions when relevant

### Important: Confirm before saving

**Write actions always require your approval inside Claude.** Before the agent records an expense, creates an invoice, or changes your books, Claude will show you exactly what's about to happen and ask for your confirmation. This is a deliberate safety feature — nothing is saved without your explicit "yes."

This means:
- You stay in control of every transaction
- Mistakes are caught before they hit your books
- You can review and modify proposals before confirming

### Troubleshooting

**I don't see AgentBook in my connectors**

Make sure the MCP server is running and your AgentBook account is active. You may need to refresh Claude or reload your browser.

**Claude says it can't access my data**

Check that you've granted Claude permission during the login flow. Go to your [AgentBook account settings](/docs/configure/business-profile) and verify that the connection is still active.

**I want to revoke access**

You can disconnect Claude from your AgentBook account under **Settings → Connected Apps**. Once revoked, Claude will no longer have access to your books.
