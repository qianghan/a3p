## Business profile & accounting basis

Your profile tunes how AgentBook categorizes, taxes, and reports. Find it under **Configure → Business profile** (or **Settings → AgentBook**).

### What to set

- **Business type** — freelancer, sole proprietor, consultant, contractor, or small business. Affects default categories and deductions.
- **Country / region** — drives tax rules and form types (e.g. US 1040/Schedule C, Canada T2125, UK, AU). This is separate from where employees are paid.
- **Currency** — your booking currency. Invoices can be quoted in another currency and converted for the books.
- **Accounting basis** — **cash** or **accrual**.

### Cash vs accrual — which do I pick?

| | Cash | Accrual |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue counts when… | payment is **received** | the invoice is **issued** |
| Expenses count when… | money **leaves** | the bill is **incurred** |
| Best for | most freelancers & sole props | businesses tracking receivables/payables |

You can view most reports on either basis on demand with a **basis toggle** — the setting here is just your default.
