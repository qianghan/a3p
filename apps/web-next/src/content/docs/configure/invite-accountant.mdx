## Invite your accountant

AgentBook is self-serve AI bookkeeping — and when you want a human, you bring your **own** accountant. Invite them to a secure portal without giving up your login.

### Send an invite

1. Go to **Accountant → Invites** (or ask the agent to *"invite my accountant"*).
2. Enter their name and email and send. They receive a link to a scoped portal — no AgentBook account required.
3. The invite link expires; you can revoke access at any time.

If email delivery isn't configured for your workspace yet, you can copy the portal link and send it yourself.

### What your accountant can do

- Review your P&L, balance sheet, and tax estimate.
- See the AI's proposed fixes and, if you enable write access, apply corrections.
- Request specific documents from you through the portal.

### Document requests

When your accountant asks for something (a receipt, a prior return), it appears in your **Document requests** list and you'll be nudged — so nothing falls through the cracks at filing time.
