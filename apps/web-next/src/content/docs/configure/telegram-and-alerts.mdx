## Telegram & alerts

AgentBook goes where you are. Connect Telegram to capture expenses, ask questions, and get proactive alerts without opening the app.

### Connect Telegram

1. Go to **Configure → Notifications** and start the Telegram connection.
2. Open the bot it links you to and send the code (or tap **Start**).
3. Your Telegram chat is now tied to your account — messages there act on your books.

From Telegram you can snap a receipt photo, say *"log $12 parking"*, or ask *"what did I spend on software this quarter?"* — the same agent, same data.

### Proactive alerts

The agent watches for things worth flagging and nudges you:

- **Unusual spend** — a charge well above your normal for that category.
- **Missed deductions** — categories where you likely have deductible spend you haven't logged.
- **Reminders** — bills coming due, quarterly tax set-asides, an invoice gone unpaid.

Tune what you receive under **Configure → Notifications**. Web push can be enabled too, once notification keys are set for your workspace.
