## Bank connection & sync

### Managing accounts

Under **Expenses → Bank accounts** you can link additional accounts, see each one's status, and disconnect any account (which revokes access and clears the stored token).

### Automatic vs manual sync

- A daily background sync keeps everything current — you don't need to do anything.
- To pull the latest right now, hit **Sync** (or ask the agent to *"sync my bank"*). A first-time sync imports available history; if there's a lot, it continues over the next few syncs.

### Reconciliation

Imported transactions are matched to your invoices and expenses:

- **High-confidence matches** are reconciled automatically (an invoice is marked paid, a payment recorded).
- **Uncertain matches** are flagged as *needs review* so you can confirm.
- **Duplicates** are prevented — re-syncing never double-imports.

### Personal accounts

Link personal accounts too. Flag transactions as business or personal so the agent keeps your tax picture clean while still giving you the whole-life view.
