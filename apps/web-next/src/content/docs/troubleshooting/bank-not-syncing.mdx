## My bank isn't syncing

Most sync issues are the bank connection needing a refresh. Work through these in order.

### 1. Run a manual sync

Go to **Expenses → Bank accounts** and press **Sync** (or ask the agent to *"sync my bank"*). This pulls anything pending immediately.

### 2. Check the account status

If an account shows **needs attention** or **disconnected**, your bank has expired the connection (common after a password change or every 90 days). Click **Reconnect** and sign in again — your history and categorizations are preserved.

### 3. Missing older transactions?

A first-time connection backfills history over several syncs rather than all at once. If the past year isn't complete yet, run **Sync** a few times or wait for the daily sync to finish draining it.

### 4. Transactions imported but uncategorized

That's expected — items the agent isn't sure about wait in your **review queue**. Confirm them there and it learns for next time.

Still stuck? Ask the agent *"why isn't my bank syncing?"* — it can report the account's last sync time and status.
