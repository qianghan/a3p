## Sign-in & access issues

### Forgot your password

On the login screen choose **Forgot password?** and enter your email. You'll get a reset link — set a new password and sign in.

### Continue with Google or GitHub

If your workspace has SSO enabled, use **Continue with Google / GitHub** on the login screen. Sign in with the same email you use elsewhere and AgentBook links it to your account automatically.

### "Session expired" or bounced to login

Sessions last a while but do expire. Just sign in again — your data is untouched. If it recurs immediately, clear the site's cookies and retry.

### Didn't get a verification or reset email

Check spam first. Delivery depends on your workspace's email setup — if a verified sender domain isn't configured yet, some messages may not arrive. Ask your administrator, or use SSO to sign in in the meantime.

### Account suspended

If you see an account-suspended message, an administrator has paused access. Contact them to reactivate it.

Still blocked? Reach your workspace administrator — from **Admin → Users** they can reset access, reactivate an account, or adjust roles.
